Hello there!

I am a researcher at CEA, specializing in neutron imaging. My work focuses on developing advanced imaging setups, which include CMOS and SPAD cameras coupled with various detector systems, conducting measurements and performing analysis of experimental data.

I completed my PhD in October 2024, during which I designed and built advanced X/gamma-ray cameras for the visualization of radioactive sources in a wide field of view. My research bridged applications in both the nuclear industry and high-energy astrophysics.
